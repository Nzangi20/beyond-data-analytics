Frequently Asked Questions

Got questions? We've got answers about our programs, materials, and support structure.

What is the duration of the courses?

Our standard programs range from 6 to 12 weeks depending on the selected topic. Self-paced enrollment allows you to complete the materials as fast or slow as your schedule allows.

Do I get a certificate upon completion?

Yes, all programs include a verified certification pathway. Self-study paths require you to pass all modular exams to order your certificate, while tutor-led paths include evaluation within the price.

What are the payment methods and options?

We accept M-Pesa payments, standard credit card, and direct bank transfers. Cohort courses also support installment layouts.

How does the mentorship program work?

Tutor-guided pathways connect you with a dedicated industry mentor who conducts weekly project evaluations, answers questions inside the discussion channels, and offers guidance.

Is there career support available?

Yes. Guided cohort paths include portfolio creation reviews, resume optimization sessions, and direct guidance on tech job interviews.
